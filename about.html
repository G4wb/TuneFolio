About TuneFolio

Your Personal Music Companion

Our Mission

TuneFolio is designed to help music lovers discover, organize, and enjoy their favorite tracks across multiple genres. We believe in creating a personalized music experience that grows with your taste.

Features

Smart Library

Organize your favorite songs into custom playlists and access them anytime, anywhere.

Genre Exploration

Discover new music through curated genre collections and personalized recommendations.

Liked Songs

Keep track of your all-time favorites with our dedicated Liked Songs playlist.

Easy Discovery

Find exactly what you're looking for with our intuitive search and filtering system.
